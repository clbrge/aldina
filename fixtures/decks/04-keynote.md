# The End of the Search Box
### Maya Iyer · FutureStack 2026

## Every interface you have ever used was built on one assumption:

## that **you** would do the searching.

![](visual:fullbleed-crowd)

## That assumption is dead.

## In 2026, software searches for you.

> "The best interface is no interface."

## Three shifts.

## From queries → to intentions.

## From results → to answers.

## From tools → to agents.

![](visual:fullbleed-abstract)

## The companies that win won't have better search.

## They won't have search at all.
