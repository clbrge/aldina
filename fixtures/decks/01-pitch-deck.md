# Lumen
### Logistics exception handling that runs itself
Nadia Brooks · Founder & CEO · June 2026

## The problem
Mid-sized freight operations lose **20+ hours a week** reconciling delivery exceptions by hand.

- Exceptions buried across email, EDI, and phone
- Missed claims windows quietly bleed revenue
- No single view of what went wrong, or who owns it

## The cost
**$1.4B** in unrecovered freight claims every year in North America alone.

## Our solution
Lumen turns exception chaos into a single, self-routing dashboard.

- Real-time exception detection across every channel
- Automatic routing to the right owner
- Claims filed before the window closes

## How it works
1. Ingest events from TMS, EDI, and email
2. Detect and classify exceptions automatically
3. Route, resolve, and file — with a full audit trail
