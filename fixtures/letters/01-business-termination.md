Dear Mr. Chen,

We are writing to formally notify Precision Components LLC of Hollis Manufacturing's decision to terminate Supply Agreement #PC-2291, effective ninety (90) days from the date of this letter, in accordance with Section 12(b).

This decision follows an extended internal review of our component sourcing and is not a reflection of any single performance issue. During the notice period, we ask that you:

- Fulfill all open purchase orders dated on or before June 25, 2026;
- Suspend production against any forecast releases scheduled beyond that date;
- Return all consigned tooling itemized in Exhibit C of the agreement.

Our accounts payable team will issue a final reconciliation statement within thirty (30) days of the effective termination date. Any outstanding balances will be settled per the original payment terms.

We have genuinely valued our six-year relationship with Precision Components and would be glad to discuss a structured transition for the affected product lines.

Please direct any questions to me directly or to our procurement office.
